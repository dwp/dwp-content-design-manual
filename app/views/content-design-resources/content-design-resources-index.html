Content Design case studies

  • SSE Redesigning their energy bills
  • SSE ending energy bill confusion

    • These two articles show how SSE redesigned their energy bills to make them better for users. They found that users were only interest in a couple of pieces of information (the price and when they needed to pay) so the letter was simplified to make these as clear as possible.

  • Why we removed the only feature everyone liked in our new advice tool – Katherine Vaughan

    • This article by Citizens Advice talks about the reasons why they removed their ‘quick links’ feature despite the fact it was popular. It’s a good example of how using data and analysing websites as a whole can take you beyond just ‘what people like’ to create a better user experience.

  • Creating better content for users with low health literacy – NHS Digital

    • Mark Robinson and Ana Savic talk about how they designed content for users with low health literacy.
