Reviewing our work

Tools and advice on how to get your work reviewed and how to review other people's work.

  • DWP Content Design 2i Trello board

    • You'll need to be signed in to Trello to use this. This was set up for each service team to 2i their content. '2i' means to give a piece of content a second 'eye' – that is, check it against certain criteria and proofread it – before it's published or shared.

  • How to broadcast a content crit in Slack

    • In the past, we've broadcast content crits via Slack for the whole community to join - here's how to do it.

  • Content crits – Sarah Richards

    • Sarah Richards was the head of content design at GDS and pioneered it as a profession in government. She established content crits as a part of the design process so worth reading about it from her.

  • The virtues of a content crit – Natalie Shaw

    • Another good explainer about how to run content crits and what the benefits of them are.
